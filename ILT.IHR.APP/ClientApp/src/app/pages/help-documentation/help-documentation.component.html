View/Add AppraisalDetails:
  1. Click on Appraisal left side menu.
  2. When Appraisal Status is Assigned and Clicked on edit icon, will display appraisal form to be filled by the employee.
  3. Employee Need to select Employee Rating from 1 to 5 for all the objectives
  4. Employee Need to select Employee Rating from 1 to 5 and Enter the Employee Comments for each Goal from pervious appraisal.
  5. Employee Need to Enter Current Year Goals.Additional Goals can be added by clicking the add button.Minium of three and max of six goals.
  6. After Click the Save button data will be saved Successfully, but not submitted for review.
  7. On Click of Submit Button, Appraisal Status Changes to PendingReview and will be submitted to the Manager/Reviewer.
  8. When Appraisal Status is PendingReview and Clicked on edit icon, will display appraisal form to be filled by the manager.
  9. Manager Need to select Manager Rating from 1 to 5 for all the objectives.
  10. Manager Need to select Manager Rating from 1 to 5 and Enter the Manager Comments for each Goal from pervious appraisal.
  11. Manager Need to Enter Current Year Goal.Additional Goals can be added by clicking the add button.Minium of three and max of six goals.
  12. Manager Need to provide Manager FeedBack.
  13. After Click the Save button data will be saved Successfully, but not submitted for Final Reviewer.
  14. On Click of Submit Button, Appraisal Status Changes to Pending Final Review and will be submitted to the Final Reviewer.
  15. When Appraisal Status is Pending Final Review and Clicked on edit icon, will display appraisal form to be filled by the Final Reviewer.
  16. FinalReviewer Will view all the ratings and comments given by the Employee/Manager and provide the Final Comment.
  17. After Click the Save button data will be saved Successfully,but not submitted for Complete.
  18. After Click the Submit Button, Appraisal Status Change to Complete
